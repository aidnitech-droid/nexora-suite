1. Introduction

Nexora Suite ("we," "us," "our," or "Company") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our website and application.

Developed by: Hardik Gajjar, Aidni Global LLP
Email: support@aidniglobal.in

2. Information We Collect

A. Information You Provide Directly:
  • Account Registration: Name, email address, phone number, company name, country/region
  • Payment Information: Billing address, payment method (processed securely via third-party gateways)
  • Business Data: Customer information, invoices, financial records you upload to our platform
  • Communication: Support tickets, feedback, chat messages, survey responses
B. Information Collected Automatically:
  • Device Data: IP address, browser type, operating system, device identifiers
  • Usage Data: Pages visited, features accessed, time spent, login frequency
  • Cookies & Tracking: Session cookies, analytics, performance monitoring
  • Location Data: Approximate location based on IP address (with your consent)
C. Third-Party Sources:
  • Payment processors (Stripe, PayPal, etc.)
  • Identity verification services
  • Analytics providers (Google Analytics, Mixpanel)

3. How We Use Your Data

We use collected information for:

  • Providing and maintaining Nexora Suite services
  • Processing payments and managing your subscription
  • Customer support and technical assistance
  • Sending transactional emails (confirmations, updates, alerts)
  • Improving our platform through analytics and feedback
  • Detecting and preventing fraud and security issues
  • Complying with legal obligations and law enforcement requests
  • Sending marketing communications (only with your consent)

Legal Basis (GDPR/Similar): Legitimate business interests, contract performance, legal compliance, consent

4. Data Sharing & Disclosure

We do not sell your personal data. We may share data with:

  • Service Providers: Cloud hosting (AWS), payment processors, email services
  • Legal Requirements: Law enforcement, government agencies, court orders
  • Business Partners: With your explicit consent for integrations
  • Successor Entities: In case of merger, acquisition, or bankruptcy

Data Processors: All third parties signing Data Processing Agreements (DPA) with strict confidentiality obligations.

5. Data Security

We implement enterprise-grade security measures:

  • Encryption: TLS 1.3 in transit, AES-256 at rest
  • Authentication: bcrypt password hashing, multi-factor authentication (MFA)
  • Infrastructure: Cloud providers with ISO 27001 certification
  • Access Controls: Role-based access, principle of least privilege
  • Monitoring: Real-time security monitoring and intrusion detection
  • Backups: Encrypted, automated daily backups with off-site replication
  • Incident Response: 24/7 incident response team, breach notification within 72 hours

Note: While we implement strong security, no system is 100% secure. We are not liable for unauthorized access beyond our control.

6. Your Rights & Choices

A. Data Subject Rights (GDPR):
  • Right to Access: Request a copy of your data
  • Right to Rectification: Correct inaccurate information
  • Right to Erasure: Request deletion of your data (right to be forgotten)
  • Right to Restrict Processing: Limit how we process your data
  • Right to Portability: Export your data in a standard format
  • Right to Object: Opt-out of processing for specific purposes
B. US Consumer Rights (CCPA):
  • Right to know what personal information is collected, used, and shared
  • Right to delete personal information collected
  • Right to opt-out of "sales" (sharing for business purposes)
  • Right to non-discrimination for exercising CCPA rights
C. Canadian Rights (PIPEDA):
  • Right to access and correct your personal information
  • Right to request deletion
  • Right to withdraw consent
D. To Exercise Your Rights:

Email support@aidniglobal.in with your request. We will respond within 30 days.
