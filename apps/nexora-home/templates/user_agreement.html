User Agreement

Last Updated: December 8, 2025 | Effective Date: January 1, 2026

1. User Categories 2. Individual User Rights & Obligations 3. Organization Admin Rights 4. Account Usage Policies 5. Data Management 6. Support & SLA 7. Acceptable Use Policy 8. Penalties & Enforcement

1. User Categories

A. Individual/Free User:
  • No credit card required
  • Limited to 3 team members
  • Access to basic modules
  • Community support only
  • Subject to data retention of 90 days post-cancellation
B. Professional/Paid Subscriber:
  • Unlimited team members
  • Access to all 25+ modules
  • Priority email support (24 hours)
  • API access included
  • Data retention: 30 days post-cancellation
C. Enterprise User:
  • Custom configuration and deployment
  • Dedicated account manager
  • SLA guarantee (99.9% uptime)
  • Phone + email support 24/7
  • Custom data retention agreements

2. Individual User Rights & Obligations

A. Your Rights:
  • Right to create one personal account
  • Right to access features included in your subscription tier
  • Right to export your personal data
  • Right to delete your account
  • Right to report abuse or security issues
  • Right to file a data subject request (GDPR/CCPA)
B. Your Obligations:
  • Provide accurate, truthful information during registration
  • Keep your password confidential and secure
  • Notify us immediately of unauthorized access
  • Use only for legitimate business purposes
  • Not share your account with others
  • Comply with all applicable laws
  • Not engage in harassment or abusive behavior

3. Organization Admin Rights

A. Admin Permissions:
  • Manage team member invitations and roles
  • Access all data within your organization
  • Configure organization settings and modules
  • View and manage billing
  • Request data exports for your organization
  • Delete the organization and data
B. Admin Responsibilities:
  • Ensure all team members comply with this agreement
  • Monitor and control access to sensitive data
  • Implement data governance policies
  • Maintain backup copies of critical data
  • Report security issues immediately
C. Sub-Admin Limitations:
  • Sub-admins have limited permissions as configured by the main admin
  • They cannot create new admins or delete the organization
  • They cannot change billing information

4. Account Usage Policies

A. Account Sharing:
  • One user per login credential
  • Do not share credentials with other individuals
  • Inviting team members creates individual accounts for each person
  • Account sharing violates terms and may result in suspension
B. Multi-Tenancy & Organization Limits:
  • Each organization is isolated and secure
  • One login email per user account
  • You may belong to multiple organizations (separate accounts recommended)
  • Organizations cannot be transferred to other users
C. Testing & Sandbox Accounts:
  • Demo/sandbox accounts are for testing only
  • Do not use test data in production
  • Test accounts may be purged without notice
  • Demo mode blocks delete operations (for safety)

5. Data Management

A. Data Ownership:
  • You retain 100% ownership of your business data
  • We do not claim any rights to your data
  • You may export data anytime in CSV or JSON format
  • We recommend maintaining your own backups
B. Data Retention:
  • During Active Subscription: Data stored indefinitely
  • After Cancellation: Free users 90 days, Paid users 30 days
  • Data Export Window: 7 days after cancellation
  • Permanent Deletion: After retention period ends
C. Data Backup & Recovery:
  • We maintain encrypted daily backups
  • Backups are stored across multiple geographic regions
  • Recovery requests honored within 48 hours
  • For Enterprise: guaranteed 99.99% backup success rate
D. Data Restrictions:
  • Do not store sensitive personal data (SSN, passwords, credit cards directly)
  • Do not store data for illegal purposes
  • Do not upload malware or exploit code
  • We reserve the right to delete prohibited content

6. Support & Service Level Agreement

A. Support Tiers:
Tier Response Time Support Channel Availability
Free 72 hours Community forum Business hours (EST)
Professional 24 hours Email, Chat 24/7
Enterprise 1 hour (Critical) Phone, Email, Chat, Slack 24/7
B. SLA Guarantees (Enterprise only):
  • 99.9% uptime guarantee
  • Service credits for downtime exceeding threshold
  • Quarterly business reviews
  • Dedicated account manager
C. Support Exclusions:

Support does not include:

  • Custom feature development
  • Data recovery from user error (if backups available)
  • Training or consulting services (available as add-on)

7. Acceptable Use Policy

A. Prohibited Activities:

You will NOT:

  • Use Nexora for illegal activities or violate laws
  • Attempt unauthorized access or hacking
  • Deploy malware, viruses, or exploits
  • Scrape data or use automated bots without permission
  • Harass, threaten, or abuse other users
  • Spam or send unsolicited communications
  • Compete with Nexora using our platform
  • Reverse engineer or decompile our software
B. Content Standards:
  • All business data must comply with applicable laws
  • No hate speech, obscenity, or discriminatory content
  • No child abuse, exploitation, or CSAM content
  • No copyright or trademark infringement
  • No fraudulent or deceptive content
C. Performance Standards:
  • Do not consume excessive bandwidth or CPU resources
  • Do not make excessive API calls beyond your tier limits
  • Do not overload our infrastructure with spam

8. Penalties & Enforcement

A. Warning:
  • First violation may result in a written warning
  • You have 7 days to cure the violation
  • Failure to cure may result in suspension
B. Suspension:
  • Accounts may be suspended for serious violations
  • We will provide notice and reason (except emergency cases)
  • You can appeal within 14 days
  • Suspended data is retained for 30 days
C. Termination:
  • Accounts may be terminated for repeated violations or illegal activity
  • Data is deleted per retention policy
  • No refunds issued for terminated accounts
  • You may appeal termination decisions
D. Appeal Process:
  1. Send appeal request to support@aidniglobal.in within 14 days
  2. Provide context and explanation of your position
  3. Our team will review and respond within 7 business days
  4. We reserve the right to make final decisions

This User Agreement is supplementary to our Terms of Service and Privacy Policy. All documents taken together constitute the complete agreement.
